About Us

1st International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Vision is being organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, School of Technology, Pandit Deendayal Energy University, Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India from September 24-26, 2021. The conference is proposed with a basic theme towards 'Artificial Intelligence and Machine Vision'. AIMV 2021 will provide researchers to interact and collaborate under one umbrella for recent developments, applications and research outcomes on Computer Vision, Machine Intelligence, Human Computer Interaction and Data Science. The conference has Plenary Talks and Expert Panel discussions as well.
